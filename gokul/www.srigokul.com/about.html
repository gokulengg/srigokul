Welcome to Sri Gokul Engineering Works

Established in 1987, Sri Gokul Engineering Works in India has established itself as a leading manufacturer, supplier, and trader of heavy machining machines such as precision machines, CNC machined components, and parts like power transmission equipment and turbine compressor blades. Located in Hyderabad, Telangana, our company is renowned for providing high-quality products and exceptional service to our valued customers.

With a highly experienced team, boasting an average of around 30 years of expertise per member, we are dedicated to delivering top-notch products and outstanding customer service. Whether it's sales and service, mechanical engineering, or machining, we are committed to exceeding your expectations in every aspect of our work.

Our workshop is equipped with state-of-the-art machines for precision work. Over the years, we have successfully manufactured numerous components and parts for various industries including transportation, mining, and oil. We specialize in heavy machining, fabrication, and repairs, delivering quality products to clients nationwide. Feel free to explore our gallery to see some examples of our manufacturing capabilities.

Since our inception in 1980, we have been serving various industries with precision machining and custom metal fabrication services. We take pride in ensuring quality and reliability in all our endeavors.

Through our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction, we have forged strong relationships with our clients over the years, earning their trust and loyalty.

At Sri Gokul Engineering Works, our commitment is to provide you with total quality and reliability. Whether you require custom metal fabrication, heavy machining, or gearing solutions, you can rely on us to meet your needs with excellence.
